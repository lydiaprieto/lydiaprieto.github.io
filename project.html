sobre el projecte: About the Project:

This project engages communities in co-creating knowledge around being an informal caregiver. Through workshops, interviews, and creative methods, we explore issues that matter to those affected to conduct better research and bring social impact. In this section I will reflect upon participatory methods, opportunities and good practices based on my experience.

Els objectius de la recerca participativa: / Goals of participatory research:

  • * Empower participants to share their stories
  • * Develop actionable insights
  • * Promote collaborative knowledge production
